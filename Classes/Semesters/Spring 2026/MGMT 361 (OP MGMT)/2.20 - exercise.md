1. The two causes of waiting lines are:
    

- When expected demand exceeds expected supply for a limited period. People arrive faster than they can get served.
    
- Variability in arrivals and/or service times, which can cause waiting even when average utilization is well below 100%.
    

2. Coefficient of variation 
    

= standard deviation ÷ mean.

- (a) Mean = 5 minutes: 
    
- CV=5/5=1.0
    
- CV=5/5=1.0.
    
- (b) Mean = 30 minutes: 
    
- CV=5/30=0.17
    
- CV=5/30=0.17.
