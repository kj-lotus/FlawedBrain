Resource Utilization
	identify the bottleneck - How busy is each resource
	Suppose the process is operating at full capacity
	Resource Utilization = ==Process Capacity /  Resource Capacity== 
		ie. student 2 is bottle neck 10 units / min = process capacity
		Resource Capacity is the specific stage's process capacity
			Stage 1: 10 unit / min /30 unit / min = 33%
			Stage 2: 10 unit / min /10 unit / min = 100%
			Stage 2: 10 unit / min /20 unit / min = 50%
Idle Time

Labor Cost
	Measure how efficient you are using labor
		How much you pay per resource
		Direct Labor cost = Total wage / process capacity
