## Why Agency Law Matters

Corporations are artificial persons — essentially a stack of papers (articles of incorporation). Since a corporation cannot physically act, it needs real individuals to act on its behalf, which is exactly where agency law comes in. ## Core Definitions

1. An agreement (expressed or implied) between principal and agent
    
2. The agent enters into contracts with **third parties**

## The Three Categories of Workers

This is one of the most important distinctions in the transcript — the precise classification determines liability, tax obligations, and benefits.

## Employee (Not an Agent)

- Works under the employer's control
    
- Does **not** enter into contracts with third parties on behalf of the employer

## Independent Contractor (Freelancer)

- Does work on your behalf, but you **do NOT control the means** (the how/when/where of the work)
    
- Cannot be an agent; cannot be an employee

## Tax Liability

- Only applies in **employee relationships**, not in pure agent/independent contractor situations
    
- Employer must pay **7.65% (half of Social Security/FICA)** for employees
    
- Independent contractors pay the **full Social Security amount themselves**
    
- Employers also owe benefits (healthcare, dental, vacation) to employees — making employees **35–50% more expensive** than independent contractors at the same hourly rate[Chapters-37-38-Agency-Law-Video-Transcript-1.pdf](https://ppl-ai-file-upload.s3.amazonaws.com/web/direct-files/attachments/118389950/f20e4658-cfdb-42fa-848a-48f8b972edc0/Chapters-37-38-Agency-Law-Video-Transcript-1.pdf?AWSAccessKeyId=ASIA2F3EMEYE3CGVVJCR&Signature=oI33UPre%2BKk3qCE6OiKhVPIW1kQ%3D&x-amz-security-token=IQoJb3JpZ2luX2VjEAYaCXVzLWVhc3QtMSJGMEQCIAuakEsdPNxmLhY1%2FCFqHQK39sGChqUlG%2FrsYrIZZpOLAiAREMdr2%2B%2B0H5mOd7VMkrO53rxKJvuphH4Yg2sysfTqNyr8BAjP%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F%2F8BEAEaDDY5OTc1MzMwOTcwNSIMDL90oliBtoVbQyovKtAETHzBc8AR2Gj5WigNh7Ok0RdG0SDh%2FejB2YjMicEdcxbjhDtSTXahrVVp3miMqNllsIgaTiA%2BKlYFUS%2FwRM0tSBtUbWiZ20tA5EnM%2BV3HMD9elO6MuPSDestXEE4cw9arQ5J53dVKfUyfzuzvDl7a3%2FUjWGyKOpTu0GThFwOgfm%2FEJv4eCzsALEdWGqtwTYwACmqDYqe13BLQzhLCPB03WHDKTDcDru82YuBo%2FwgdejYs6wUyVPLx85IS4w0a4f1Vo14SqyNN0DSYfGTcLzOIEry395GmIe%2FkfCt3RVrnaExycvVb2gi33LqKP56KWbrfDEkJaImfco4ma49KDC85xeu4SPBIz4ymRRMAnjiYuKBew682D0na5wE32X55N%2FoPoUOMLegylRQi7qlnVWv388c3bHfDMixq5r7isldiWb56PtQlm757QPbdiDu242vs34DtScLvXXTPGvqdxtu5IX1CwpKdwNvK7ff%2F1DXg5IbsUOWOHerJzReBwI3uBCIOiz0Kk5sX1hGo7Invgy%2F6q4q%2Bmxa%2FBS8ZsZuGQz2YXLnU9zNJ3SWUSbP4zXL8aPdPfDHs7gA9UTuoIwZzJfn04iETKyRMS%2F1nNUixujgt%2FVa7%2BQjHqDhOllmy3EKkBGlLO%2B93%2Fi%2BayK%2BCmfKBAmdry49ZH%2F9KHAJyLm3cFHw542niQoYrDMzbJSXz33ic2xWlr6HsEhdaMJjvr16xrRQ3iWBu4oJpYys9Ih5odpUsPv%2BX6b%2BRzMcCMfcVxcl1i2XdYs7xGjpTiHoZ6OHV2cSvHzDsy%2FfPBjqZAbL5Er0xkkk01H441kkBRO4uZOrKNyjX%2FceFdbhTfdUByADeGsMHhiuAy3uCBS3woHNrPoxkCB7NsCJiXAMTRicFcK3ovsutBsV%2B7un6sLJENs%2B%2FG7647xbTBPUz82UMPXLNW8MYUFBqoGgw54wKmSHMT9G9x1ZYzTCNrfg%2Bo6pJ4cMd0s%2FWoBNF9dw%2Fs%2F6SJCfYuwsEisa87Q%3D%3D&Expires=1778250293)
