### Counterfactual Thinking
What does it mean for one thing to cause another
	Cause and effect relationship
		Change in X leads to a change in Y
	Connection is temporal
		thing in X has to happen before effect happens in Y
	Variable - multiple states of being
		if its in one state why react a certain way
		which means Y is also a variable
		if X and Y is always the same value there is no cause and effect relationship

What is a Counterfactual thinking
	Thinking process in which one thinks about alternative scenarios and outcomes
		helps leaders how different conditions and external factors may produce different results
	We only get to observe one outcome
		the COUNTERFACTUAL is the other outcome: what would have happened if you made the other choice
	To make the best choices we must infer what the counterfactual is since we can never observe it
Abraham Wald's Memo
	Wald is tasked to help armor a plane
		He makes a heat map of bullet penetrations on plane
		majority of bullets hit wings and tail wings
			Wald said that those spots are a place where you can take a hit and make it back, but places like cockpit and engine if hit would not make it back
				Therefore armoring the engine was the best decision
