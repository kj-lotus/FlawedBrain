### Company Question Ideas:

### Airbnb

Strategic Question:  
How should Airbnb respond to global regulation and local housing restrictions?

1. Is Airbnb's core business model broken, or does it simply need to outrun the regulators long enough to evolve into something they can't stop?
    

  

2. Does Airbnb have a responsibility — and the strategic leverage — to actively shape housing policy in the cities it operates in, or does political engagement ultimately do more damage to the brand than the regulations themselves?
    

  

3. How should Airbnb respond strategically to increasingly strict short‑term rental regulations in major global cities over the next 3–5 years, in order to sustain growth while addressing concerns about housing affordability and community impact?
    

  

4. How should Airbnb reposition itself publicly and operationally in response to the political narrative that short-term rentals worsen urban housing affordability, in order to reduce regulatory risk in major U.S. cities?
