##### Transaction Cost Economics
What is it?
	the expenses incurred when participating in a market, separate from the production price of a good or service
Firms have different Transaction Costs
Firm Advantage
	Command and control
	Coordination
	Transaction specific investments
	Community Knowledge
Firm Disadvantages
	Administrative Costs
	Low powered incentives
	Principal agent problem
		a conflict of interest arising from asymmetric information where an agent (e.g., manager) makes decisions on behalf of a principal (e.g., owner), but acts in their own self-interest rather than the principal's
Market Advantage
	High powered incentives
	Flexibility
Market Disadvantages
	Search Costs
	Opportunism
	Incomplete contracting
		Specifying & Measuring performance
		information asymmetries
	Enforcement of contracts
