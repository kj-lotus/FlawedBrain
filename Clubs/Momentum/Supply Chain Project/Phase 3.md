**The Operational Fix**

Week 9-11

This phase is where we can add the most value with SCM. We propose strategic plans to meet the reduction goals we found in phase 2.

There will most likely be 3 fixes to mix and match with.
1. SKU Rationalization (Pareto Analysis)
	1. Problem: They sell 10,000 Items but 80% of sales come from 20% of the items
	2. Fix: Cut bottom 15% of underperforming SKU (Stock Keeping Units)
	3. Result: Immediate reduction in holding costs and warehouse clutter.
2. Vendor Consolidation and Negotiation
	1. Problem: Buying from 500 different suppliers prevents bulk orders and discounts
	2. Fix: Consolidate the top 50 suppliers and renegotiate payment terms (Targets DPO increase)
	3. Result: Lower COGS and improved cash flow
3. Network optimization (Hub & Spoke)
	1. Problem: Inventory is fragmented among 500 stores and gets "trapped"
	2. Fix: Centralize slow moving items in regional hubs
	3. Result: Lower safety stock requirements.

Now we need to model the improvement and to do this we have a few cases.
	Base Case: No change.
	Bear Case: Ops improve slightly (DIO reduces slightly)
	Bull Case: Large or near full transformation (DIO reduces significantly and gets close to or matches gold case)
