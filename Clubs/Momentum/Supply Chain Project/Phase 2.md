**The Diagnosis**

Week 5-8

We will be conducting an Outside - In analysis. This is so that we can quantify the efficiency gap and figure out what state their supply chain is in.

To visualize all this data We will build an Excel model which tracks the following metrics from the past 5 years.

1. DIO (Days in Inventory Outstanding) How long does stuff sit on the shelf? 
	1. Goal: Identify if the trend is getting worse.
2. DPO (Days Payable Outstanding): How fast do they pay suppliers? 
	1. Insight: If they pay in 20 days but competitors pay in 60, they are losing a "free loan" from suppliers.
3. Inventory to Sales Ratio: Is inventory growing faster than revenue?

Next we need information around risk factors and what their previous supply chain plan was.
To do this we search the 10-K annual report for "Risk Factors" and "Properties" sections
We want to know a few details
	1. Warehouse Footprint
		1. Do they lease or own
		2. Do they have too many distribution centers
		3. Are they located in places with easy distribution
	2. Write downs
		1. We need to look for "Provision for obsolete inventory"
		2. This proves they are buying the wrong stuff
	3. Supplier Concentration
		1. Do they reply heavily on one area?

Essentially we need a lot of information in order to create mitigation plans and a new SCM plan.

Finally we have to do some money calculations

		Finance - Calculate exact dollar value of 1 days worth of inventory
			This way we can calculate the impact "Reducing DIO by just 1 day saves $X"
