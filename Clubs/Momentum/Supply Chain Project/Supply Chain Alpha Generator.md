WHY: The main outcome of this project is to find a company with high amounts of "trapped cash" and find ways to help company lower their Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)

	CCC = Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO)+ Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)− Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) 

The Logic behind this
	If a company holds less inventory (DIO) and pays suppliers later (DPO) it can generate free cash. This cash can then be used to buy back stocks or pay dividends which in turn increases share price.


Instead of taking a Private Equity approach, we will find a company with a bloated, inefficient Supply Chain and we calculate how much shareholder value we can unlock by fixing it. This is not just analyzing a stock but instead simulating an Operational Turnaround.

Why Momentum Needs This:

Differentiation: It breaks the monotony of standard Investment Banking pitches.

True Collaboration: It is the only project that forces Finance majors (modeling) and Supply Chain majors (operations) to actually work together.

Recruitability: It gives members a "Hero Story" for interviews: "I identified $50M in working capital savings and modeled the impact on stock price."
